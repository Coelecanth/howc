Dog Leg Gallery

What is the Dog Leg Gallery?

Dog Leg Gallery is a distinctive virtual and physical art space. The physical is nestled within the corridors that connect the various rooms of the House of Wild Cards. This innovative gallery celebrates local talent by showcasing and promoting the works of emerging artists. Offering a platform for exposure, Dog Leg Gallery provides art enthusiasts with the opportunity to discover and purchase exciting, original pieces. Its unique location creates an intimate and dynamic environment for both artists and visitors.
The virtual galley is a show case for the work that is completed within the House of Wild cards by the students them selves, it shows all the various art faculties and shows the journey that the students undertake transforming their skills as artists, and their tramsformative progression.
