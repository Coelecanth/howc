What is the Flamingo Go-go Theatre?

Flamingo Go-go Art is a vibrant art studio that inspires creativity across all age groups. The youth and teen programs offer engaging lessons for students aged 6 to 12, introducing them to famous artists and techniques in a fun and interactive way. For older students aged 12 to 16, Arts Awards programs provide a student-led approach, where projects are tailored to individual interests, encouraging self-expression and growth. Adults can explore their artistic side during evening classes, whether they are beginners, advanced artists, or looking to reignite a passion from their past. Arty Parties provide a unique, bespoke celebration for any occasion, from birthdays to casual gatherings with friends.
